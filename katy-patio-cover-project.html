Project Details

A Complete Backyard Transformation

This family in Katy had a large backyard but couldn't use it during the hot summer months. Their existing patio was too small and had no shade. They wanted a space where they could cook, eat, and entertain without fighting the Texas heat.

What We Built

  • Extended Patio Cover: Solid-roof structure that doubled their usable outdoor space and provides complete shade.
  • Built-in Grill Station: Natural gas grill with stainless steel finish and side burner.
  • Stone Countertops: Durable granite surfaces for food prep and serving.
  • Bar Seating: Counter-height area where guests can sit and socialize while the homeowner cooks.
  • Under-counter Storage: Weatherproof cabinets for grilling supplies and outdoor dinnerware.
  • Ceiling Fans: Two ceiling fans installed for air circulation on still days.

The homeowners now use their backyard almost daily, even in July and August. It's become their favorite room in the house—even though it's technically outside.
