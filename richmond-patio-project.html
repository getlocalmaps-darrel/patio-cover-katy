Project Details

An Outdoor Chef's Dream Kitchen

This Richmond couple had been cooking on a portable grill for years and wanted to upgrade to a permanent outdoor kitchen. They cook for their family several times a week and host neighborhood gatherings regularly.

What We Built

  • L-Shaped Layout: Efficient work triangle for cooking with plenty of counter space.
  • Built-in Gas Grill: High-end 42" grill with four burners and infrared rotisserie.
  • Granite Countertops: Heat-resistant and easy to clean, in a color that complements the home.
  • Double Access Doors: Large storage compartments for propane, grilling tools, and supplies.
  • Refrigerator Space: Under-counter cutout for outdoor-rated refrigerator (owner-supplied).
  • Bar Ledge: Extended counter on one side for bar stools and casual dining.

The project included running a gas line from the house and installing electrical for the refrigerator and future lighting. Everything was permitted and inspected to code.
