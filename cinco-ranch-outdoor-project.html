A Grilling Station Built for Entertaining

The homeowners in this Cinco Ranch neighborhood loved to host weekend gatherings but were tired of working from a standalone grill with no counter space. They wanted a permanent grilling station that would be the centerpiece of their backyard entertaining.

What We Built

  • Stone-Finished Base: Natural stone veneer that complements the home's exterior.
  • Built-in Gas Grill: Premium 36" grill with rotisserie and infrared burner.
  • Granite Countertops: Plenty of prep space on both sides of the grill.
  • Storage Cabinets: Weatherproof stainless steel doors with interior shelving.
  • Side Burner: Perfect for sauces, sides, or keeping food warm.
